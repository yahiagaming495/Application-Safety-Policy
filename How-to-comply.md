# How to Comply with SSCD

Adopting the Software Safety Consent Declaration is simple and demonstrates your commitment to transparent security practices.


### Add the Badge to your application
Add the badge [here](https://github.com/yahiagaming495/Software-Safety-Consent-Declaration/SSCD.svg) to your application

### Step 2: Reference the Policy

Add this statement to your documentation, website, or privacy policy:

> "This project follows the Software Safety Consent Declaration, ensuring transparent security and privacy practices. [View the full policy](https://github.com/yahiagaming495/Software-Safety-Consent-Declaration/blob/main/SSCD)."


Once you've implemented SSCD:

1. **Update your project's documentation**
2. **Add your project to our [USERS.md](USERS.md) file** via Pull Request
3. **Share your experience** in our [Discussions](https://github.com/yahiagaming495/Software-Safety-Consent-Declaration/discussions)

## Check if the application using SSCD is legitamate

To verify a project's SSCD compliance:

1. Check for the SSCD badge in their documentation
2. Verify they link to the SSCD policy  
3. Review their security practices
4. [Report issues](https://github.com/yahiagaming495/Software-Safety-Consent-Declaration/issues) for any discrepancies
